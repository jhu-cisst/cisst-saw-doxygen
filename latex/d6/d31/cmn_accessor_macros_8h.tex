Macro to declare a data member and implement accessor methods. This macro creates 4 different accessors, two to read and two to write. For example, the following call\-: 
\begin{DoxyCode}
\hyperlink{cmn_accessor_macros_8h_a383ef2cc4a35f586e7bbe9f55837140b}{CMN\_DECLARE\_MEMBER\_AND\_ACCESSORS}(\textcolor{keywordtype}{double}, Timestamp)
\end{DoxyCode}
 will create the 4 following methods\-: 
\begin{DoxyCode}
\textcolor{keywordtype}{double} & Timestamp(\textcolor{keywordtype}{void});
\textcolor{keyword}{const} \textcolor{keywordtype}{double} & Timestamp(\textcolor{keywordtype}{void});
\textcolor{keywordtype}{void} SetTimestamp(\textcolor{keywordtype}{double} newValue);
\textcolor{keywordtype}{void} GetTimestamp(\textcolor{keywordtype}{double} & placeHolder);
\end{DoxyCode}
