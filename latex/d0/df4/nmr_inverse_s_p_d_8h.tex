Computes the inverse of a symmetric positive definite matrix. 

This computes the inverse of a symmetric positive definite (S\-P\-D) matrix. The $ N \times N $ matrix is assumed S\-P\-D and only the lower triangular is considered in the computation. The upper triangular is assumed to be symmetric even though it might not be as it is not used to compute the inverse.
